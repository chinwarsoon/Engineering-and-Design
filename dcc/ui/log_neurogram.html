Help

Navigation: Pan by dragging, zoom with scroll wheel.

Click a node to see details in the right panel.

Double-click a node to focus and highlight its connections.

Focus Selected limits the graph to the selected node neighborhood.

Double-click background to reset the view.

Filter: Toggle node types in the Graph panel or use Advanced Filter.

Edge filters: Toggle relationship types in Advanced Filter.

Search: Type in the search bar to find nodes.

Search filters graph: Turn this on in Settings to show matches plus neighbors.

Timeline: Use the slider at the bottom to filter by date.

Export: Download the current view as PNG or filtered data as JSON.

Themes: Click 🎨 to switch between 5 color themes.
